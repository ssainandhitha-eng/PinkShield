‚ö° Quick Features to Try

1. Emergency SOS (No Login Required!)

  • Click the red "Emergency SOS" button on Welcome Screen
  • Or press Ctrl + E anywhere in the app
  • Or tap 5 times rapidly (if enabled in settings)

2. Multi-Language Support

  • On Welcome Screen, click the language dropdown
  • Choose from: Tamil, English, Hindi, Malayalam, Telugu, Kannada

  • During Sign Up, enable Voice Navigation
  • Enable Vibration Alerts for haptic feedback
  • Try Large Icons Mode for better visibility
  • Set up Tap-Pattern SOS for quick emergency access

  • Use Tab key to navigate between elements
  • Press Ctrl + H to go to Home Screen
  • Click bottom navigation tabs: Home, Map, Community, Profile
